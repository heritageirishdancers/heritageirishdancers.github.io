About Us

Heritage Irish Dance Company

Heritage Irish Dance began in 2017 under the Komotion Dance Program in Danville, PA. In September 2020, we moved to Bloom Health and Fitness in Bloomsburg, PA. HID is the only member of Rince Tuatha Nua in the Central Pennsylvania area, and offers traditional Irish dance instruction to all ages and levels. Classes at HID foster collaboration, musicality, and fitness. Students can have fun with friends, while also learning and growing in a positive environment.

Jennifer Kline

MEET MISS JENN:

Jennifer Kline is the founder and sole instructor of Heritage Irish Dance. Growing up, she danced and competed with Butler Academy through 2008. During her time at the studio, she quickly rose through the levels, and a life-long passion for dance began. In 2015, she started teaching Irish dance at the Komotion Dance Program in Danville, PA. Two years later, she began Heritage Irish Dance as a competition class. She is currently working toward earning her TCRG, a certification for Irish dance teachers.
