Classes

Dance with Us!

Our classes provide traditional Irish step training in a collaborative and positive environment. Decide between casual or competitive options, and see below for the schedule and levels currently offered.

Fitness


Irish step allows dancers to practice agility, coordination, and endurance.

Confidence


Develop technique, find friends, and grow as a dancer!

Musicality


Learn how to dance to different styles and speeds of music.

Levels

Pre-Beginner

The pre-beginner class is for ages 3-5. Dancers will learn basic technique, rhythm and timing with fun age appropriate activities. Lots of skipping and jumping!

Register

Beginner A

This class is for dancers ages 6+ who are brand new to Irish dance, or who have had less than a year of instruction from another teacher. In this class, students will learn basic steps and technique.

Register

Beginner B

This class is for students who have had more than one year of previous Irish dance instruction but have not completed the Beginner competition level or are new to Heritage. Dancers will work on the 4 soft shoe dances and begin working on hard shoe.

Register

Intermediate

Dancers who are currently in Intermediate level at competition. Students will dance reel, light jig, single jig, slip jig and traditional speed treble jig and hornpipe.

Register

Novice/Prizewinner

Dancers currently competing at Novice and/or Prizewinner level. Novice and Prizewinner level dances will be reel, slip jig, slow speed treble jig, slow speed hornpipe, and traditional sets.

Register

Champion

Dancers currently competing at Champion level (including Preliminary Champion). Champion level dances include reel, slip jig, slow speed treble jig, slow speed hornpipe, traditional sets, and non-traditional sets.

Register
