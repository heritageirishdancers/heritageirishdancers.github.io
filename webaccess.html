Heritage Irish Dance Website Accessibility Policy



At Heritage Irish Dance, we strive for accessibility. We want to ensure that everyone, including persons with disabilities, has full and equal access to our content.

We welcome any feedback on your experience with heritageirishdancers.github.io. Email us at heritageirishdance@gmail.com with any questions or comments.

Our website complies with ADA requirements as listed here.

Heritageirishdancers.github.io is compatible with most Internet browsers including Chrome, FireFox, and Safari. However, it may not perform optimally on Internet Explorer and older browser versions.

All accessibility features were coordinated and incorporated by Abril Xu.

For more information on web accessibility and legal requirements, look into these resources: ADA.gov, Section508.gov, Accessible.org, and W3.org/WAI.
